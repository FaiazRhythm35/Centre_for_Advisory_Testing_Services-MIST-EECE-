CATS-MIST (EECE) Documentation for Client

Written Documentation

How to sign up and login
  • Go to https://eece-cats.mist.ac.bd/
  • Click on Request for service
  • If you are a new client, sign up
  • If you are from any organization, select ‘Organization’. Write the name of your organization and your role in that organization
  • If it’s your personal project, select the ‘Self’
  • If you have an existing account, login
Dashboard Overview

In the Dashboard, you will get the overview and status of all of your lab test and consultancy.

In Lab Test:

  • Create lab test
  • Upload the documents for the lab test
  • Make bank payment
  • Submit the sample at the CATS-MIST (EECE)
  • You will get an SMS and the status in you Dashboard will be Ready To Collect when the report is ready to collect

In Consultancy:

  • Create consultancy and the amount will be added by the authority
  • After adding the amount, you come to MIST to make the payment at the bank
  • You will get an SMS and the status in you Dashboard will be Ready To Collect when the report is ready to collect

How to make payment

Bank Payment

Please see the How to Pay page for complete bank account details.

How to collect report

  • You can check the status in your dashboard.
  • You will get an SMS and the status in you Dashboard will be Ready To Collect when the report is ready to collect.
  • Then you can come and collect the report.
